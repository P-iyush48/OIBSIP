Tribute to Prime Minister Narendra Modi

Honoring his vision, leadership, and dedication to India

Early Life

Narendra Modi was born on 17 September 1950 in Vadnagar, Gujarat. From humble beginnings, he worked at his family’s tea stall and later joined the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS), which shaped his early political and social outlook.

Political Journey

Modi rose through the ranks of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), serving as Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014. In May 2014, he became the Prime Minister of India and was re-elected in 2019.

Key Initiatives

  • Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Clean India Mission)
  • Digital India & Make in India
  • Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (Financial Inclusion)
  • Infrastructure expansion and rural electrification

Vision & Leadership

Modi advocates for Atmanirbhar Bharat (Self-Reliant India), promoting innovation, entrepreneurship, and technology-driven growth. His leadership has strengthened India’s global presence.
